Legal Services

Dr. Stevens has been retained in over 65 matters to serve as a litigation consultant and testifying expert witness in disputes in the life sciences and business methods. His practice falls in two areas:

  • Patent infringement; and
  • Breach of Contract

    • He is an academic affiliate of Analysis Group, Inc., an international economic consulting firm based in Boston.

    Unless lost profits are being sought, Dr. Stevens will determine the total damages owed. If lost profits are sought, he will work with an economist and determine the reasonable royalty that should apply to that portion of the accused sales that are determined not to be subject to lost profits.
